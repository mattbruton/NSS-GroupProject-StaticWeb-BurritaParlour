The Meat of the Story

We believe that happy animals make happy meat. Not only do our animals live organic and free range lifestyles, we provide them with housing in loft apartments located in the Gulch. The animal's dwellings are stocked with local craft beer and produce organically grown in Middle Tennessee. Prior to being processed, they are blessed and purified by an animal spirit healer.
