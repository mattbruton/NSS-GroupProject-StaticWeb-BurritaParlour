Ever wondered what a $24 burrito tasted like?

Probably pretty close to the $6 burrito you had last night at Las Palmas. However, thats where the similarities end. Each one of our lil Burritas is hand crafted by a loving, bearded denizen of East Nashville. We are committed to using the most expensive, niche ingredients to inflate your bill while delivering the least amount of value. As well, we will berate you with an over-indulgence of subculture with every experience. We guarantee you will leave feeling satisfied with an underlying sense of betrayal.
